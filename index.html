Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a city rich in history and culture. During my visit, I explored some of the most iconic tourist places, including:

  • Red Fort: A historic fort built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.
  • India Gate: A war memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers.
  • Qutub Minar: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world.
  • Lotus Temple: A stunning Bahá'í House of Worship shaped like a lotus flower.

Delhi is a city that beautifully blends the old and the new, offering a unique experience to every visitor.
